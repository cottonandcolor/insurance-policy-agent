SYNTHETIC HOMEOWNERS POLICY — PLAN A (PUBLIC SAMPLE FORM)
Jurisdiction: TX
Carrier: Synthetic Mutual

Section I — Declarations
Coverage A Dwelling: $350,000
Policy Deductible: $2,000 all perils

Section IV — Perils Insured
Standard homeowners perils covered subject to exclusions below.
Water backup from sewers or drains: Covered up to $10,000 sublimit.

Section IV-A — Flood
Flood damage is EXCLUDED unless Endorsement HO-FLD is attached to the policy.
Surface water flooding is not covered under the base form.

Section VI — Exclusions
We do not cover loss caused by flood, surface water, or waves unless HO-FLD is attached.
Earth movement is excluded.

Section VIII — Optional Endorsements
HO-FLD Flood Endorsement: optional; adds flood coverage up to Coverage A limit.

Annual Premium (synthetic): $1,850
